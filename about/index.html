About Bible Timeline Quiz

Bible Timeline Quiz is an interactive puzzle game where you arrange biblical events in chronological order. This page explains how the game works and what features it offers.

How the Game Works

1. Select a Level

The game offers 5 difficulty levels that affect which events you'll encounter. Higher levels include more complex and less well-known biblical events. You can choose your preferred level before starting the game.

2. Choose a Time Period

You can select from preset time periods or customize the exact range of years. The major periods include:

  • Genesis to Patriarchs (Creation to ~1513 BCE)
  • Conquest (~1513 to ~1070 BCE)
  • Kingdom & Exile (~1070 to ~280 BCE)
  • Jesus & Apostles (~2 to 98 CE)
  • All Periods (~4026 BCE to 98 CE)

The narrower the time range you select, the more challenging the game becomes, as events will be closer together in time.

3. Choose the Number of Events

Select how many biblical events you want to arrange in order. Options range from 3 (Easy) to 20 (God Mode). The more events you choose, the higher the difficulty.

4. Use Hints to Learn

If you arrange the events incorrectly, you can view hints after checking your answer. The hints show the biblical book and verse references for each event, helping you understand where in the Bible each event occurs and improve your knowledge of biblical chronology.

Game Content

The game currently includes over 200 biblical events spanning from the creation account to the first century CE. Each event is based on widely accepted biblical chronologies and historical sources.

For deeper learning about biblical history and chronology, explore our articles and resources page, which provides detailed explanations and links to relevant biblical passages.

If you'd like to suggest additional biblical events or have feedback about the game, please feel free to contact us via our Contact page. We'd love to hear your suggestions!

We hope this game enhances your understanding and appreciation of the Biblical Timeline. Enjoy arranging the events and discovering the sequence of major biblical moments!
