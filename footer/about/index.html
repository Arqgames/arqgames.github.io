About ARQ Games

ARQ Games is a small independent project that creates simple, interactive quiz games you can play directly in your browser.

Our goal is to make learning enjoyable by combining clear design, intuitive interaction, and carefully selected content. No registration or installation is required — just open the page and start playing.

Currently, ARQ Games focuses on timeline-based quiz games, such as the Bible Timeline Quiz, where players arrange historical events in the correct chronological order. These games are designed for educational and entertainment purposes and are suitable for casual play.

ARQ Games does not promote or represent any specific organization or belief. The content is provided as general educational material and interactive entertainment.

We plan to gradually add more quizzes and browser-based games covering a variety of topics.

Thank you for visiting and enjoy playing.
