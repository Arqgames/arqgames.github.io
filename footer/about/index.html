About BTLQ

BTLQ (Bible Timeline Quiz) is an interactive quiz game designed to help you learn biblical history through chronological ordering of events.

Our goal is to make learning enjoyable through clear design and intuitive interaction, while helping users study history and reinforce what they learn. No registration or installation is required — just open the page and start playing.

BTLQ specializes in timeline-based quiz games where players arrange biblical events in chronological order. The game is designed to help users review biblical history repeatedly and retain it more effectively.

BTLQ does not promote or represent any specific religious organization or belief. The content is provided as general educational material and interactive entertainment.

We plan to continue improving BTLQ with more biblical events and enhanced features.

Thank you for visiting. We hope you enjoy both learning and playing.
