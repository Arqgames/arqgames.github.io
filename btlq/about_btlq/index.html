About Bible Timeline Quiz

Bible Timeline Quiz is an interactive puzzle game where you arrange biblical events in chronological order, making learning about biblical history both engaging and fun.

The concept is simple: given a set of biblical events from different periods, players must arrange them in the correct order. This helps develop a deeper understanding of biblical chronology and the relationships between major events across different historical periods.

The game offers multiple difficulty levels and customizable options. You can select specific periods (Genesis to Patriarchs, Conquest, Kingdom Period, Exile & Return, Jesus & Apostles, or All Periods) and choose the number of events to arrange (3, 5, 8, 12, or 20). A time attack mode is also available for those seeking an extra challenge.

The events included in the quiz are based on widely accepted biblical chronologies and historical sources. The game supports both English and Japanese, making it accessible to a global audience.

Learning through gameplay helps reinforce knowledge of biblical events, their sequence, and their historical significance in a memorable and enjoyable way.

This game is designed for educational purposes, suitable for students, church groups, and anyone interested in biblical history. It combines entertainment with learning, providing a unique way to explore the Bible's historical timeline.

We hope you enjoy exploring the Biblical Timeline and discovering the sequence of significant biblical events!
