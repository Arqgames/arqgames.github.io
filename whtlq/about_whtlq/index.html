About World History Timeline Quiz

World History Timeline Quiz is an interactive puzzle game where you arrange world history events in chronological order. This page explains how the game works and what features it offers.

How the Game Works

1. Select a Level

The game offers 5 difficulty levels that affect which events you'll encounter. Higher levels include more complex and less well-known historical events. You can choose your preferred level before starting the game.

2. Choose a Time Period

You can select from preset time periods or customize the exact range of years. The major periods include:

  • Ancient Civilizations (~3000 to ~500 BCE)
  • Classical & Medieval (~500 BCE to 1500 CE)
  • Early Modern Era (1500 to 1900 CE)
  • Modern Era (1900 to Present)
  • All Periods (~3000 BCE to Present)

The narrower the time range you select, the more challenging the game becomes, as events will be closer together in time.

3. Choose the Number of Events

Select how many world history events you want to arrange in order. Options range from 3 (Easy) to 20 (God Mode). The more events you choose, the higher the difficulty.

4. Use Hints to Learn

If you arrange the events incorrectly, you can view hints after checking your answer. The hints provide context and details about each historical event, helping you understand the significance of major moments in world history and improve your chronological knowledge.

Game Content

The game includes major world history events spanning from ancient civilizations to the present day. Each event is based on widely accepted historical sources and chronologies.

If you'd like to suggest additional historical events or have feedback about the game, please feel free to contact us via our Contact page. We'd love to hear your suggestions!

We hope this game enhances your understanding of world history and chronology. Enjoy arranging the events and discovering the sequence of major historical moments!
